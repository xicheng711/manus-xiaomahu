# Figma Design Reference Notes

## Visual Style
- **Rounded colorful cards** for quick access grid (pink, blue, purple, green)
- **Line-style icons** inside colored squares/circles (not filled emoji, but thin stroke icons)
- **Tab bar**: light background, active tab has colored fill background square, icons are line-style
- **Tab icons**: house (首页), clipboard (每日打卡), pill capsule (用药), open book (日记)
- **Profile avatar**: orange circle with person icon (top right)
- **Color scheme**: green primary, soft pastel backgrounds
- **Cards**: white with soft shadow, rounded corners ~16-20px
- **Typography**: bold Chinese titles, lighter subtitles

## Tab Bar Icons (line style)
- 首页: house outline
- 每日打卡: clipboard outline
- 用药: pill/capsule outline
- 日记: open book outline

## Quick Grid Icons (white line icons on colored bg)
- 用药提醒: pill/capsule (pink bg)
- 护理日记: heart with bookmark (blue bg)
- 家庭共享: people/group (purple bg)
- AI 助手: brain (green bg)

## Back Button Style
- Simple "← 返回" text or chevron left with text
- Positioned top-left
- Should be smooth with press animation

## Key Design Principles
- Colorful but not overwhelming
- Line icons (not emoji) for professional feel
- Generous padding and rounded corners
- Soft shadows on cards
